Updating Availabilities

  • Click [Employees] tab on Teachworks
  • On the right, "Download Availabilities"
  • Copy/Paste Availabilities.xls → "Availabilities" Google Spreadsheet BUT DON'T INCLUDE HEADER (row 1)
  • Employees without availabilities filled out on Teachworks will not show up

Updating Subjects/Employees

  • Click [Employees] tab on Teachworks
  • On the right, "Download Employees"
  • Copy/Paste everything (include header row) Employees.xls → "Subjects" tab of Google Spreadsheet
